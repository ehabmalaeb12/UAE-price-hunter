About UAE Price Hunter

UAE Price Hunter is a UAE-focused price comparison platform designed to help users find the best available prices across trusted online stores.

Our mission is simple: save users money and time by comparing prices transparently, while rewarding users with cashback points for eligible purchases.

UAE Price Hunter does not sell products directly and does not handle payments or deliveries. All purchases are completed on partner websites.

This app is independently operated and built with compliance, transparency, and user trust as top priorities.
