UAE Price Hunter is a price comparison platform that helps users discover the best available prices across third-party online stores in the United Arab Emirates.

We do not sell products directly and do not handle payments, deliveries, or customer service for any store.

2. Price Accuracy Disclaimer

Prices displayed in the app are for reference only and may change at any time on the merchant’s website. Final prices are determined solely by the store.

3. Affiliate Disclosure

UAE Price Hunter participates in affiliate marketing programs. When users click on a store link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to the user.

4. Cashback & Points System

Cashback points shown in the app are pending and subject to confirmation by the merchant.

Points:

  • Are earned only on eligible purchases (minimum AED 30)
  • Have no cash value outside the app
  • Can only be used within UAE Price Hunter
  • May be adjusted or canceled if orders are refunded or rejected
